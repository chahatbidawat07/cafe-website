COCO CAFE

ABOUT OUR STORY

We will open our cafe on January 2, 2030. As a student, it was always my dream to
open my own vintage-style cafe, not only just for the business or profit, but to
own a place to relax after dealing with all the headaches of the day, I want to 
be in a place where I can find peace and I want everyone to enjoy this peace and 
relax for awhile, far away from the chores.

OUR MISSION

  • To to provide high-quality coffee at reasonable price
  • To create vintage vibes and beautiful photo spots that bring smiles to people's faces
  • To raise funds that support the education of children in need

WHY CHOOSE US

  • Vintage and cozy atmosphere
  • Freshly brewed coffee
  • Delicious desserts
  • Friendly service
  • Comfortable study and reading space
